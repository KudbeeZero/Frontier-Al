FRONTIER — Battle Arcs: Animated Attack Lines on the Globe
Pass: Globe-BattleArcs

Context:
When a battle is pending, draw a glowing animated arc on the globe
surface from the attacker's territory to the defender's parcel.
The arc pulses and has a moving "projectile" dot that travels along it.
When the battle resolves the arc fades out. Multiple concurrent arcs
render simultaneously.

Arc origin = the attacker's first owned parcel lat/lng (from
player.ownedParcels[0] looked up in the parcels array).
Arc destination = battle.targetParcelId lat/lng.
Arc midpoint = lifted above the globe surface using a bezier control
point scaled by the great-circle distance between the two points —
longer distances = higher arc.

Only pending battles render arcs. Resolved battles fade out over 1.5s.
