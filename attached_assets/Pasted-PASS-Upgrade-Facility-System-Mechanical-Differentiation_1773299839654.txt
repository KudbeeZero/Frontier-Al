CONTEXT
════════════════════════════════════════
Four systems need fixing:

1. RADAR does nothing — costs 45I 35F and has zero mechanical effect.
2. FACILITIES are identical — Blockchain Node, Data Centre, AI Lab all give
   the same output at the same cost. No strategic choice exists.
3. "MINE" upgrade modifies yieldMultiplier (same as Yield upgrade) — two
   upgrades that do the same thing with different price tags.
4. "FORTRESS" in UPGRADE_COSTS overlaps with the Fortress building, is
   strictly worse, and confuses players.
5. The UI does not show what upgrades do, crystal is missing from the stat
   bar, biome yield profile is invisible, and the collapsed Upgrade button
   silently triggers a defense upgrade with no player choice.
