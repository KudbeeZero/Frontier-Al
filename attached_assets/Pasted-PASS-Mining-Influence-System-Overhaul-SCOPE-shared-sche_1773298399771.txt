════════════════════════════════════════
CONTEXT
════════════════════════════════════════
Three systems need fixing:

1. Mining yields are too low — crystal rounds to zero at almost any richness,
   biomes all produce the same resource types, storage fills in 28 minutes,
   and plots hit the depletion floor after 5 hours with no recovery.

2. Influence repair rate (2 pts/day) means one lost battle takes 7.5 days to
   recover. Players will be permanently damaged. The repair timer is also
   wired to lastFrontierClaimTs (wrong timestamp). Influence also has no
   effect on resource mining — it only gates FRONTIER generation, making it
   an invisible stat during normal play.

3. The influence UI card is an unstyled naked div that shows a raw number
   with no % sign, no progress bar, and no warning when yield is blocked.
   It does not match the other stat cards.
