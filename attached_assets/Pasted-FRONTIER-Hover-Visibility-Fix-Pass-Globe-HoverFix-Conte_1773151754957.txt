FRONTIER — Hover Visibility Fix
Pass: Globe-HoverFix

Context:
Hovering over any globe plot currently shows nothing visible.
Two bugs:

BUG 1: The useFrame loop only iterates animatedIndices (owned/
selected/battle plots). Hovered tiles that are unowned are never
updated in the animation loop, so hover color is never applied.

BUG 2: HOVER_COLOR = "#0a2040" is near-black. With AdditiveBlending
on the hex mesh, adding near-black contributes almost zero light,
making the hover invisible even when it does trigger.

DO NOT TOUCH: any server file, routes.ts, storage.ts, schema,
GameLayout.tsx, or anything outside PlanetGlobe.tsx.
DO NOT change raycaster logic, click handling, CameraController,
BattleArcs, MiningPulseLayer, OrbitalZoneLayer, or GlobeTerrain.

File: client/src/components/game/PlanetGlobe.tsx
