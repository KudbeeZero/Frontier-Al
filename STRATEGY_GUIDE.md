## 1. Getting Started

### First 30 Minutes Checklist

1. **Connect wallet** — Pera or LUTE, receive 500 FRNTR welcome bonus
2. **Buy your first plot** — Desert (0.2 ALGO) or Swamp/Plains (0.3 ALGO) for budget start
3. **Mine immediately** — Tap mine, wait 5 minutes, mine again
4. **Build Electricity** — Spend 30 FRNTR for +1 FRNTR/day and to unlock advanced facilities
5. **Build a Turret** — 40 Iron + 20 Fuel for basic defense (prevents easy captures)
6. **Set up mining rotation** — If you bought multiple plots, cycle through them every 5 minutes
7. **Save FRNTR** — Don't spend your remaining ~470 FRNTR yet; plan your build path first

### First Plot Selection Guide

| Budget | Recommended Biome | Why |
|--------|------------------|-----|
| Tight (0.2 ALGO) | Desert | Massive fuel production (2.5x), cheap |
| Budget (0.3 ALGO) | Swamp | Crystal bonus (2.0x), affordable |
| Standard (0.5 ALGO) | Forest | Balanced stats, good crystal (1.5x), decent defense |
| Invest (0.8 ALGO) | Mountain | Best defense (1.4x battle mod), best iron (2.0x) |

## 2. Biome Selection Strategies

### The Resource Farmer

**Goal:** Maximize raw resource output

- **Primary**: Volcanic (1.8x iron, 2.5x crystal) — the best dual-resource biome
- **Secondary**: Desert (2.5x fuel) — unmatched fuel production
- **Weakness**: Low defense on both — invest in turrets early

### The Fortress Builder

**Goal:** Build impenetrable territories

- **Primary**: Mountain (2.0x iron, 1.4x battle defense) — the premier defensive biome
- **Secondary**: Tundra (1.8x fuel, 1.2x battle defense) — solid secondary fortress
- **Build order**: Turret Lv3 → Shield Gen Lv2 → Fortress → Radar
- **Result**: Mountain fortress with maxed improvements has ~161 defender power

### The Crystal Hunter

**Goal:** Maximize crystal income for advanced upgrades

- **Primary**: Volcanic (2.5x crystal, 1.8x iron) — best value crystal
- **Risky**: Water (3.0x crystal) — highest crystal but 0.5x battle defense makes it a glass cannon
- **Budget**: Swamp (2.0x crystal, 0.3 ALGO) — affordable crystal farming

## 3. Resource Management

### Mining Rotation

With a 5-minute cooldown per plot, optimize your rotation:

| Plots Owned | Strategy |
|-------------|----------|
| 1–3 | Mine each plot sequentially, 5-min gaps |
| 4–6 | Cycle through all plots continuously — by the time you finish the last, the first is ready |
| 7+ | Prioritize highest-value plots first, skip low-richness plots when time is limited |

### Storage Optimization

Base storage is **800 units**. Prioritize Storage Depot construction on high-yield plots:

- Storage Depot Lv1: 800 → 1,000 (35 Iron + 15 Fuel)
- Storage Depot Lv2: 1,000 → 1,200
- Storage Depot Lv3: 1,200 → 1,400
- + Fortress: 1,400 → 1,450

> **Rule of thumb:** If a plot fills storage before your next collection cycle, build a Storage Depot.

### Resource Allocation by Game Phase

| Phase | Iron Priority | Fuel Priority | Crystal Priority | FRNTR Priority |
|-------|--------------|--------------|-----------------|----------------|
| **Early** (0–10 plots) | Turrets | Turrets | Save | Electricity on every plot |
| **Mid** (10–20 plots) | Shield Gens + Upgrades | Shield Gens | Advanced upgrades | Blockchain Nodes |
| **Late** (20+ plots) | Fortress builds | Fortress builds | Trading stock | Commanders + Landmarks |

## 4. Defense Strategies

### Minimum Viable Defense

For plots you can't afford to lose but don't want to over-invest in:

| Build | Cost | Defender Power (Plains) |
|-------|------|----------------------|
| Turret Lv1 | 40 Fe + 20 Fu | (1×15 + 5) × 1.0 = 20 |
| + Radar Array | 45 Fe + 35 Fu | 20 + radar (-10% to attacker) |

Total cost: 85 Iron + 55 Fuel. Radar doesn't add defense power directly but reduces incoming attack power by 10%.

### The Mountain Fortress

The strongest possible defensive setup:

```
Plot: Mountain (1.4x battle defense mod)
Defense Level: 3 (from Defense upgrades)
Turret: Lv3
Shield Generator: Lv2
Fortress: Lv1

improvementBonus = (3×5) + (2×5) + (1×5) = 30
defenderPower = (3×15 + 30) × 1.4 = 75 × 1.4 = 105

With Radar: Attacker loses 10% power before comparison
```

To break this fortress, an attacker needs:
- At minimum: 12 troops with no resources = 120 base power (barely enough)
- Comfortable: 8 troops + 100 iron + 80 fuel + Reaper commander = ~224 power

### When to Invest in Defense

- **Always defend**: Mountain/Volcanic plots (expensive to buy, hard to replace)
- **Defend if near AI**: Border plots adjacent to AI territory
- **Skip defense**: Cheap plots far from enemies during early expansion
- **Rush defense**: Any plot captured from AI (they will try to reconquest within 48h)

## 5. Attack Strategies

### Power Calculation Targeting

Before attacking, estimate whether you can win:

```
Your attack power = (troops × 10) + (iron × 0.5) + (fuel × 0.8)
Add commander bonus: × (1 + ATK%)
Apply morale if debuffed: × 0.85
Random range: ±10%
```

> **Rule:** Aim for at least **1.3x the estimated defender power** to account for the ±10% random swing.

### Scouting Protocol

1. Deploy Recon Drone (20 FRNTR) to target plot
2. Wait 15 minutes for scout report
3. Calculate defender power from report:
   - `(defLevel × 15 + improvementLevels × 5) × biomeDefMod`
4. Commit resources to exceed 1.3x that value

### Timing Attacks

- **During orbital hazard events**: Target plots with active tile hazard (-20% defense)
- **After AI reconquest failure**: AI enters morale debuff + cooldown
- **During Conflict season phase**: Pillage rate increases to 40% (from 30%)

### Pillage Optimization

Target resource-rich plots for maximum stolen goods:
- 30% of stored resources is stolen (40% during Conflict phase)
- Prioritize plots with full or near-full storage
- Desert plots accumulate fuel fast — rich pillage targets
- Volcanic plots store iron + crystal — valuable double-resource haul

### Avoiding Morale Spirals

- After 2+ consecutive losses, **stop attacking** — the morale debuff (-15% power) and attack cooldown stack
- Switch to defense or mining for 5–10 minutes to let debuffs expire
- A successful defense resets your consecutive loss counter

### Concurrent Attack Management

| Commander | Max Concurrent | Strategy |
|-----------|---------------|----------|
| Sentinel | 1 | Single focused strike — make it count |
| Phantom | 2 | Split attacks on adjacent plots — capture a corridor |
| Reaper | 3 | Multi-front offensive — overwhelm a section of the map |

### 12-Hour Lock Timer

After deploying a commander in an attack, they're locked for 12 hours. Plan accordingly:
- Don't deploy before sleep if you plan to attack in the morning
- Time major offensives when you have 12+ hours of play time ahead
- Consider minting a second commander for backup during lock periods

## 7. Special Attack Playbook

### Orbital Strike — The Fortress Breaker

- **When to use**: Against heavily fortified Mountain/Volcanic plots
- **Combo**: EMP Blast first (disable improvements 10 min) → Orbital Strike (ignores 50% remaining defense) → Standard attack with full troops
- **Cost**: 25 FRNTR + cooldown: 30 min
- **Effective against**: Any defense level — the 3.0x multiplier + 50% defense bypass is devastating

### EMP Blast — The Improvement Neutralizer

- **When to use**: Before any attack against plots with Turret/Shield Gen/Fortress
- **Combo**: EMP → standard attack within the 10-minute disable window
- **Cost**: 15 FRNTR + cooldown: 20 min
- **Effective against**: Fortress builds that rely on improvement bonuses for defense

### Siege Barrage — Area Denial

- **When to use**: Against clusters of enemy plots; hits target + up to 3 adjacent
- **Combo**: Siege Barrage on central plot → standard attacks on weakened neighbors
- **Cost**: 40 FRNTR + cooldown: 45 min
- **Effective against**: Enemy territory clusters, creating vulnerability across multiple fronts

### Sabotage — Economic Warfare

- **When to use**: Before a major offensive to cripple enemy resource production
- **Combo**: Sabotage multiple plots → wait 30 min for economic damage → attack when they can't afford repairs
- **Cost**: 10 FRNTR + cooldown: 15 min
- **Effective against**: Resource-dependent players, high-yield plots

### Cooldown Stacking

Chain different attacks for sustained pressure:
```
0:00  — Sabotage (15 min cooldown)
0:01  — EMP Blast (20 min cooldown)
0:02  — Orbital Strike (30 min cooldown)
0:03  — Standard attack
0:15  — Sabotage ready again
0:21  — EMP ready again
0:32  — Orbital Strike ready again
```

## 12. AI Faction Counter-Strategies

### vs NEXUS-7 (Expansionist)

- **Behavior**: Aggressive land grab, low readiness threshold (0.6), 1.3x aggression
- **Attack modifier**: 1.2x attack, 1.0x defense
- **Counter**: Contest their border plots early — they expand fast but don't fortify well
- **Defense**: Min defense before reconquest is only 2 — they'll reconquest anything poorly defended
- **Exploit**: They spread thin; pick off isolated plots on their border

### vs KRONOS (Defensive)

- **Behavior**: Fortifies heavily, 0.6x aggression, won't attack until defense level 5+
- **Attack modifier**: 0.9x attack, 1.3x defense
- **Counter**: Don't attack head-on — use Sabotage to weaken, then EMP + Orbital Strike
- **Defense**: Highest min defense before reconquest (5) — they wait longer to strike back
- **Exploit**: They're slow; expand around their territory and cut them off

### vs VANGUARD (Raider)

- **Behavior**: Raids weak targets with 1.4x aggression, only needs 1 defense for reconquest
- **Attack modifier**: 1.3x attack, 0.9x defense
- **Counter**: Shore up weak border plots with Turrets + Radar Arrays
- **Defense**: They may capture and abandon (raider behavior) — don't panic, they might leave
- **Exploit**: Their own plots have weak defense (0.9x modifier); strike back hard

### vs SPECTRE (Economic)

- **Behavior**: Prefers richness 60+ plots, 1.0x aggression, balanced modifiers
- **Attack modifier**: 1.0x attack, 1.0x defense
- **Counter**: Race them for high-richness Volcanic/Crystal-rich zones
- **Defense**: They won't reconquest plots with richness below 60 — cheap plots are safe
- **Exploit**: Predictable targeting — if you control all high-richness plots, they stall

### Triggering ADR (Adaptive Dominance Regulation)

When one AI faction reaches ~2,000 plots (~10% of map), others auto-escalate aggression:
- Intentionally let one faction expand to trigger inter-AI warfare
- While factions fight each other, expand into uncontested territory
- Clean up weakened faction territories after ADR battles

## 13. Sub-Parcel Strategies

### When to Subdivide

| Situation | Subdivide? | Why |
|-----------|-----------|-----|
| High-value Volcanic/Mountain plot | **Yes** | Full control bonus (+50%) on premium yields |
| Cheap Desert/Plains plot | **Maybe** | Only if you need FRNTR optimization |
| Border plot near enemies | **No** | Enemies can capture individual sub-parcels, breaking your bonus |
| Interior plot (surrounded by your territory) | **Yes** | Safe from attack, guaranteed full control bonus |

### Full Control Optimization

The +50% yield bonus for owning all 9 sub-parcels is significant:

| Plot FRNTR/day | Without Subdivision | With Full Control |
|---------------|--------------------|--------------------|
| 2 (base + electricity) | 2.0 | 3.0 |
| 6 (max facilities) | 6.0 | 9.0 |
| 6 + Dome bonus (×2) | 12.0 | 18.0 |

### Defensive Subdivision

Subdividing makes your territory harder to capture in one attack:
- Enemy must attack 9 individual sub-parcels instead of 1 macro-plot
- Each attack triggers a 10-minute battle — that's potentially 90 minutes to capture all 9
- You can defend sub-parcels individually during this time
- Even losing some sub-parcels only reduces yield proportionally (not total loss)

### Biome-Specific Subdivision Value

| Biome | Subdivision Value | Reason |
|-------|------------------|--------|
| Volcanic | **Excellent** | 2.5x crystal × 1.5 full control = 3.75x effective crystal |
| Mountain | **Good** | Strong defense + full control bonus on iron |
| Water | **Excellent** | 3.0x crystal × 1.5 = 4.5x effective crystal (and enemies hate attacking water) |
| Desert | **Moderate** | High fuel but low defense — only subdivide interior plots |

## 14. Orbital Event Exploitation

### Resource Burst Events (+50% Yield)

When a Resource Burst hits one of your plots:
1. **Deploy Orbital Satellite** immediately (+25% mining yield stacking)
2. Mine the affected plot first — the burst only lasts 10 minutes
3. Total yield during burst: base × biome × 1.5 (burst) × 1.25 (satellite) = **1.875x**
4. With Data Centre Lv3 (+15%): **2.16x normal yield**

### Tile Hazard Events — Offensive Window

When a Tile Hazard hits an enemy plot:
1. The plot loses **20% defense** in battle calculations
2. Immediately deploy a Recon Drone to assess the weakened target
3. Time your attack to land during the 8-minute hazard window
4. Combine with EMP Blast for devastating defense reduction:
   - EMP disables improvements
   - Hazard reduces remaining defense by 20%
   - Orbital Strike ignores 50% of what's left

### Dark Matter Farming During Impacts

Orbital impact events create a 0.3% Dark Matter drop chance on any biome:
- Mine all your plots during impact windows, not just the affected one
- The chance applies globally during the event
- Stack with Orbital Satellites for maximum yield + Dark Matter chance
- Average: ~1 Dark Matter per 333 mines during events

## 15. Seasonal Play

### Phase-by-Phase Strategy

#### Expansion Phase (Days 1–30)

- **Priority**: Buy as many plots as possible (20% price reduction!)
- **Focus**: Desert/Plains/Swamp — bulk buy cheap territory
- **Build**: Electricity on every plot immediately
- **Welcome bonus**: 1,000 FRNTR during this phase (doubled)
- **Avoid**: Over-investing in defense — expand first

#### Conflict Phase (Days 31–60)

- **Priority**: Attack and capture enemy territory
- **Focus**: Pillage rate increases to 40% — target resource-rich plots
- **Build**: Commanders and special attacks
- **Exploit**: Double orbital event frequency — more impact events to exploit
- **Defend**: Protect your border plots — everyone is more aggressive

#### Domination Phase (Days 61–90)

- **Priority**: Secure your leaderboard position
- **Focus**: +50% global FRNTR generation — maximize token income
- **Build**: Blockchain Nodes on all plots for compounding FRNTR
- **Dome**: If you own the Orbital Alien Dome, your FRNTR generation is doubled (+100%)
- **Endgame**: Positions lock in final 24h — make your last land grabs count

### Dome Racing

The Orbital Alien Dome is the single most powerful season asset:
- First to build it claims it for the entire season
- Requires 25 plots + 800 FRNTR + 40 Void Shard + 20 Dark Matter
- Start collecting Void Shards and Dark Matter from Day 1
- Rush to 25 plots during Expansion Phase price reduction
- Build the Dome as early as possible for maximum season benefit

### Long-Term Investment

The world persists between seasons — everything carries forward:
- Landmarks built in Season 1 are still there in Season 2
- Territory, improvements, sub-parcels all persist
- Long-term players compound advantages across seasons
- Focus on infrastructure early (facilities, landmarks) and competition late (leaderboard pushes)

## 16. Advanced Scenarios

### Scenario: Breaking a Mountain Fortress

**Situation:** Enemy has a Mountain plot with Turret Lv3, Shield Gen Lv2, Fortress, defense level 3.

```
Defender power: (3×15 + 30) × 1.4 = 105
With Radar: your attack power -10%
```

**Solution (Full Combo):**
1. **Scout**: Deploy Recon Drone (20 FRNTR) — confirm defenses
2. **Sabotage**: Halve yield for 30 min (10 FRNTR) — reduce resource accumulation
3. **EMP Blast**: Disable improvements for 10 min (15 FRNTR) — removes 30 defense power + Radar
4. **Orbital Strike**: 3.0x damage, ignores 50% defense (25 FRNTR) — devastating with improvements down
5. **Attack**: Within 10-min EMP window, send full troops with Reaper commander

**Cost:** 70 FRNTR + troops + resources
**After EMP:** Defender power drops to (3×15 + 0) × 1.4 × 0.5 (Orbital Strike) = **31.5**
**Your Reaper attack (5 troops + 50 iron + 50 fuel):** (50 + 25 + 40) × 1.3 × 3.0 = **448.5**

Result: Overwhelming victory.

### Scenario: Economic Snowball

**Goal:** Maximize FRNTR generation for compound growth.

1. **Buy 10 Desert plots** (0.2 ALGO each = 2.0 ALGO total)
2. **Build Electricity on all 10** (300 FRNTR) — now generating 20 FRNTR/day
3. **Build Blockchain Node Lv1 on all 10** (1,200 FRNTR) — generating 40 FRNTR/day
4. **Wait 30 days** — accumulate 1,200 FRNTR
5. **Build Blockchain Node Lv2 on 4 plots** (1,080 FRNTR) — generating 44 FRNTR/day
6. **Continue compounding** — reinvest all FRNTR into facility upgrades

**Month 1:** 20 FRNTR/day → 600/month
**Month 2:** 40 FRNTR/day → 1,200/month
**Month 3:** 50+ FRNTR/day → 1,500+/month

### Scenario: AI Faction Farming

**Goal:** Repeatedly capture AI plots to farm pillaged resources.

1. **Identify weak AI border plots** — look for low defense near your territory
2. **Attack with minimum force** — just enough to win, save resources
3. **Pillage 30% of stored resources** — AI plots accumulate resources over time
4. **Hold through 6-hour grace period** — AI can't reconquest yet
5. **If AI reconquests within 48h** — the plot is cheaper for you next time (AI faces +25% cost escalation)
6. **Repeat** — the cycle costs AI more each time, costs you less

> **Warning:** This works best against VANGUARD (raider — may abandon anyway) and worst against KRONOS (high defense threshold, will counter-attack hard).

### Scenario: Sub-Parcel Lockdown

**Goal:** Maximize yield through full subdivision control.

1. **Choose 3 high-value Volcanic plots** (interior, away from borders)
2. **Wait 4 hours per plot** (hold requirement for subdivision)
3. **Subdivide all 3** (27 total sub-parcels)
4. **Build facilities on each parent plot** — max FRNTR generation
5. **Full control bonus: +50% yield on all 3 plots**
6. **Total yield**: 3 plots × 6 FRNTR/day × 1.5 = **27 FRNTR/day** from just 3 plots

### Scenario: Season Dome Rush

**Goal:** Claim the Orbital Alien Dome first.

**Pre-Season Prep:**
1. Accumulate 40+ Void Shards (mine Tundra/Water heavily)
2. Farm 20+ Dark Matter during orbital events
3. Save 800+ FRNTR

**Season Start (Expansion Phase):**
1. Immediately buy plots to reach 25 (use 20% price reduction)
2. Build Electricity on the Dome target plot
3. Build Orbital Alien Dome as soon as requirements are met
4. **Result:** +100% FRNTR generation for the entire season + 5% reward pool bonus

### Scenario: Dark Matter Monopoly

**Goal:** Corner the Dark Matter market through event farming.

1. **Own multiple plots across different biomes** — more mines = more chances
2. **Keep Orbital Satellites active** — +25% yield and ready for events
3. **Monitor orbital event notifications** — mine immediately during impacts
4. **Build Quantum Forge** — convert surplus minerals into needed types
5. **Craft Legendary loot boxes** — guaranteed 1–3 Dark Matter per box
6. **Trade surplus Dark Matter** — it's the rarest resource, name your price
